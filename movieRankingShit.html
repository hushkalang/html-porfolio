Here are 5 top movies, widely considered among the best in cinema history:

  1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

    Genre: Drama

    Why it's great: It's consistently ranked as one of the best films ever made, praised for its direction, performances, and inspiring themes.


  2. The Godfather (1972)

    Genre: Crime, Drama

    Why it's great: A cinematic masterpiece, known for its incredible storytelling, memorable characters, and cultural impact.


  3. The Dark Knight (2008)

    Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

    Why it's great: Praised for Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker, its complex narrative, and its profound exploration of heroism and morality.


  4. Schindler's List (1993)

    Genre: Biography, Drama, History

    Why it's great: A deeply emotional and historically significant film that combines powerful performances and an important message about human compassion.


  5. Pulp Fiction (1994)

    Genre: Crime, Drama

    Why it's great: Known for its non-linear narrative, sharp dialogue, and unforgettable characters, it revolutionized independent filmmaking.
